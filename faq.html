Frequently Asked Questions

What is ValorPoint?

ValorPoint is a platform designed to reward users for referring new members. You earn NGN1,500 for every user you successfully refer who completes their registration.

How does the referral system work?

When you register, you receive a unique referral code. Share this code with friends. When a new user signs up using your code, NGN1,500 is added to your balance.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can request a withdrawal from your dashboard once you reach a minimum withdrawal threshold (e.g., NGN5,000). Funds are typically processed within 24-48 hours.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a NGN3,000 registration fee. NGN1,500 of this goes to the referrer, and NGN1,500 helps support the platform's operations.
