How ValorPoint Works

Step 1: Register

Sign up using your email and password. You can also enter a referral code if someone invited you.

Step 2: Get Your Referral Code

Once registered, you'll receive a unique referral code and link on your dashboard. This is your key to earning!

Step 3: Share and Earn

Share your referral link or code with friends, family, and your network. When they register using your code and pay the activation fee, you earn NGN1,500!

Step 4: Track Your Progress

Your dashboard shows your total balance and a list of all the users you've successfully referred.

Step 5: Withdraw

Request a withdrawal once you meet the minimum balance. Your earnings will be sent directly to your account.
